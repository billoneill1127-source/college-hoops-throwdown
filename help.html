College Hoops Throwdown
How to Play
▶
Play Game

Pick any two teams and play a live game. There are three ways to play:

Option 1 — Watch Mode
Set both teams to CPU. Sit back and watch the CPU coach both sides. Use the speed controls to fast-forward or slow things down. Great for scouting matchups or just enjoying the simulation.
Option 2 — Play Mode
Set one team to P1 (you) and leave the other as CPU. You are in control of your team without a full coaching strategy — you can still call timeouts and manage substitutions during the game, but offensive and defensive schemes are handled automatically.
Option 3 — Coaching Mode
Set one team to P1 and check the Coaching Mode box. This is the full experience:
  • Set your starting lineup before tip-off
  • Choose your offensive and defensive strategy
  • Call specific plays during the game using the Call Play button
  • Manage substitutions and strategy during timeouts
  • React to the game as it unfolds — adjust your defense, rest tired players, and make the right calls down the stretch

📅
Season

Guide your team through a 30-game college basketball season.

Getting Started

  • Click Season from the main menu
  • Select your team — you become their coach for the entire season
  • Your schedule is generated automatically with home and away games against conference and non-conference opponents

During the Season

  • Each game on your schedule can be Played (Coaching Mode) or Simulated instantly
  • Track your record and results game by game
  • Conference standings update automatically after every result

Stats Tracking

  • Full team and player statistics are tracked throughout the season for every team in your conference
  • Monitor points, rebounds, assists, shooting percentages, and more
  • Use the stats to inform your game planning and substitution decisions
🏆
Tournament

Build a bracket and crown a champion.

Getting Started

  • Click Tournament from the main menu
  • Select the teams to fill your bracket
  • Teams are seeded based on their ratings

During the Tournament

  • Each matchup can be Played or Simulated
  • Winners advance automatically — lose and your run is over
  • Simulate entire rounds at once with Simulate All
  • The bracket updates after each round until a champion is crowned
💡
Tips
  • Fatigue matters — rotate your bench during timeouts to keep your starters fresh
  • Momentum swings happen fast — call a timeout to stop a run before it gets out of hand
  • Match your defensive scheme to the opponent — zone defenses can disrupt teams that rely on the paint, while full court press works best when you need to create turnovers late
  • In Coaching Mode, your play call gives a designated player a better chance at a shot — use it to get the ball to your hot hand
  • Player stats shown during timeouts reflect the current game — use them to decide who needs a rest
